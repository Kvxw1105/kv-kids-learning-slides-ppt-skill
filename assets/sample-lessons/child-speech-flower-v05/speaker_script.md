## Continuous English Script / 英文连贯稿

Hello, everyone. My name is Mia. Today I want to tell you a small story about my little flower. I hope you will like it.

This is my flower. Her name is Pinky. She lives in a little brown pot, next to my window, where the morning light comes in.

When I first saw Pinky, she was very small. She had two green leaves and one tiny pink bud, like a little sleeping hat.

Every morning, I say, Good morning, Pinky. I look at her leaves. I check the soil with one gentle finger.

Then I give her some water. I do not pour too much. Just a small drink, drop by drop, because plants need care.

Pinky also needs sunshine and gentle rain. Sunshine makes her warm. Rain makes the soil soft. Together, they help her grow stronger.

Some days, nothing big happens. I only see the same little bud. But I wait. I know growing takes time and patience.

One afternoon, I saw something beautiful. Pinky’s bud opened like a small pink umbrella. I was surprised, and I called my mom.

I clapped my hands and smiled. I felt proud because I helped Pinky every day. Pinky made my heart feel warm.

My little flower taught me this. Small things can grow with love, water, sunshine, and time. Even a tiny bud can become bright.

Thank you for listening to my story. I hope you have a sweet day, and I hope something beautiful blooms in your heart too.

## 中文参考稿

大家好，我叫 Mia。今天我想给你们讲一个关于我的小花的小故事。希望你们会喜欢。

这是我的小花。她叫小粉。她住在一个小小的棕色花盆里，放在我的窗边，早晨的阳光会照进来。

我第一次看到小粉的时候，她很小。她有两片绿色的叶子，还有一个小小的粉色花苞，像一顶正在睡觉的小帽子。

每天早上，我都会说：早上好，小粉。我看看她的叶子，再用一根手指轻轻碰一碰泥土。

然后我给她浇一点水。我不会倒太多。只是一点点，一滴一滴，因为植物需要温柔的照顾。

小粉还需要阳光和温柔的小雨。阳光让她暖暖的，小雨让泥土变软。它们一起帮助她长得更结实。

有些日子，看起来没有发生很大的变化。我只看到同一个小花苞。但是我会等，因为长大需要时间和耐心。

有一天下午，我看见了很美的事情。小粉的花苞打开了，像一把粉色的小伞。我很惊喜，马上叫妈妈来看。

我拍了拍手，也笑了。我觉得很骄傲，因为我每天都在照顾小粉。小粉让我的心里暖暖的。

我的小花教会了我一件事：小小的东西，也可以在爱、水、阳光和时间里慢慢长大。小小的花苞，也能变得很明亮。

谢谢大家听我的故事。希望你今天也过得甜甜的，也希望有一朵美好的小花，在你的心里慢慢开放。
