# Composition candidate engine

Commercial-premium slides should not accept the first plausible layout. For key slides, generate candidate specs:

- Candidate A: dominant character or hero image.
- Candidate B: dominant object or symbolic scene.
- Candidate C: editorial / picturebook rhythm with text as graphic form.

Score candidates 1-10 on eye path, hierarchy, emotion, novelty, readability, and continuity with neighboring pages. Pick the highest score or combine two candidates.

Avoid serial template use. If the previous slide is left-text/right-image, prefer diagonal, window, full-bleed, triptych, constellation, or close-up on the next slide.
