# Art direction contract

Create `art_direction.json` for standard-finished and commercial-premium work. It must define:

- `core_emotion`: one phrase such as gentle wonder, brave discovery, warm celebration.
- `visual_metaphor`: the governing image idea, e.g. flower as a small stage companion.
- `signature_motif`: repeatable mark or shape that makes the deck recognizable.
- `palette_story`: how colors move from opening to ending.
- `character_role`: whether the child, mascot, object, or concept leads the story.
- `page_rhythm`: low / medium / high density per slide.
- `typography_voice`: how English and Chinese should feel together.
- `forbidden_moves`: e.g. office rectangles, tiny Chinese subtitles, random stickers, decorative clutter.

Before final generation, ensure each slide has a `visual_mission` derived from the art direction.
